# Settings
On your settings page, you can set up the API credentials, your profile, notifications, and your referral code. Click *Settings* on the left menu and you will see the submenu:

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0ba6e258-02f7-427c-b831-98277b3ce103)



## AI settings

Here, you can change your settings. Those who bought a plan starting with the "Key-" word ("Key-10" or "Key-99"), can change their OpenAI or/and Anthropic key.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e4c89272-fe40-4baf-aa4e-2f2af8714d95)

<Callout>
  We store API keys as encrypted strings, so we cannot show you the key you already entered. If your key is not working and you suspect you may have entered it wrong, please clear the field and enter it again.
</Callout>

## User profile

Change your user name and the profile picture (currently, they are shown on the feedback board):

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5cf210c7-e440-48e8-86f1-1c552398fc3e)

## Notification center

Select whether you want to miss comments to your posts, reply to your comments, and new posts (by default you miss them so don't forget to come and set all three ON :).

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/79447ff6-4cff-4c9d-bc2b-d8ad6fda8f95)
