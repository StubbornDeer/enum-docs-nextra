# The payment process
When you are satisfied with a free plan, you may consider to move to a paid plan to upload more documents and get more questions to be asked.
The process is a very straightforward.
## Select a plan
Click the *Billing* menu item in the left menu, you will be redirected to the page with all available plans:

![image](https://github.com/StubbornDeer/enum-docs-nextra/assets/91156314/07d639b5-103e-42e4-bb1a-72e92a9aa838)

Select a plan you'd like to move and click the "Select this plan" button. You will be redirected to the Stripe payment page:

![image](https://github.com/StubbornDeer/enum-docs-nextra/assets/91156314/e6a7f22a-fc8d-43ea-a4bb-b005a3b65c28)

Click the *See details* to see all the plan's details. If you change your mind you can click the back button to return to the dashboard.

After you finish the payment process you will be returned to the dashboard and all the necessarily data will be saved into our database, so your Billing page may look something like that:

![image](https://github.com/StubbornDeer/enum-docs-nextra/assets/91156314/ad2557ec-a7a4-4779-80da-f2ce72e0f6bf)

## Move to another plan or cancel
In the future, if you decide to move to another plan, cancel the existing first, and then select a new one.
